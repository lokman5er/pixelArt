Pixel Art

GET CREATIVE!

Unleash your creativity with our pixel editor! Explore the endless possibilities by taking a look at the templates provided at the top of the window for inspiration. And when you're done, feel free to download your creation in various formats. Happy designing!

TOOLS & SETTINGS

Select the ideal tool for your creation, be it pencil or fill bucket. If mistakes happen, use undo/redo. Erase pixels with the eraser tool. Turn off grid lines if needed. And don't hesitate to start fresh with the trash icon. Unleash your pixel art skills!

COLORS

Get inspired with a special retro color palette, or adjust the brightness to suit your needs. If you can't find the perfect shade, the color picker with its rainbow border lets you choose from an infinite spectrum of hues. Unleash your creativity and bring your pixel art to life with a world of color at your fingertips!

EXPORT & IMPORT

When you're happy with your pixel creation, don't hesitate to export it as a JPG, PNG (with transparant background), or even a GIF for download. Additionally, you can also download the creation as a .txt file and upload it in future for editing. Show off your masterpiece to the world!
